# TommysJukebox
Not a music bot.

If you use this code, DO NOT CREDIT ME!


<b> <i> Command list for Tommy's Jukebox </b> </i>

<b>Connects the bot to the voice channel (These all do the same thing)</b>
	<p> -join </p>
	<p> -connect </p>
	<p> -j </p>

<b>Plays song</b>
	<p> -play </p>
	<p> -sing </p>
	<p> -p </p>
	
<b>Pauses song</b>
	<p> -pause </p>
	
<b>Resumes song</b>
	<p> -resume </p>
	
<b>Skips song</b>
	<p> -skip </p>
	
<b>Removes specific song from queue </b> 
	<p> -remove [INSERT NAME OF SPECIFIC SONG] </p>
	<p> -rem </p>
	<p> -rm </p>

<b>Clears queue</b>
	<p> -clear </p>
	<p> -clr </p>
	<p> -cl </p>
	<p> -cr </p>
	
<b>Displays queue</b>
	<p> -queue </p>
	<p> -q </p>
	<p> -playlist </p>
	<p> -que </p>
	
<b>Shows currently playing song</b>
	<p> -np </p>
	<p> -song </p>
	<p> -current </p>
	<p> -currentsong </p>
	<p> -playing </p>
	
<b>Disconnects bot from voice channel</b>
	<p> -leave </p>
	<p> -disconnect </p>
	<p> -stop </p>
	<p> -dc </p>
	<p> -bye </p>
